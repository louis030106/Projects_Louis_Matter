# Automatic Water Tank Shut-Off System

## Objective
Design a reliable electronic system that prevents the household water tank from overflowing by automatically shutting off the water supply when the tank is full.

## Problem
The previous mechanical level-control system was inaccurate. It frequently failed to detect when the tank reached maximum capacity, causing water overflow and unnecessary waste.

## Developed Solution
I built an electronic control system consisting of:
- a water level sensor,
- a microcontroller,
- an automatically controlled solenoid valve.

When the tank reaches its maximum level, the solenoid valve closes instantly, stopping the water flow.

## Results
- No more water overflow.
- Fully automatic and highly reliable operation.
- Minimal maintenance required.
- Complete replacement of an outdated mechanical system.
